Heat Exchanger

Shell and Tube Assembly

Project Overview

This project showcases the engineering design and development of a shell-and-tube heat exchanger assembly, modeled using Creo Parametric. The assembly includes the shell, tube bundle, tube sheets, supports, and fluid connection ports.

The design demonstrates professional assembly techniques, component integration, and industrial equipment modeling practices commonly used in thermal and process engineering applications.

The assembly reflects MechaMaster Group's expertise in mechanical equipment design, industrial CAD modeling, and the development of engineering solutions aligned with real-world manufacturing and operational requirements.
