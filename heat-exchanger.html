Heat Exchanger

Project Overview

This project presents a detailed CAD assembly of a Shell-and-Tube Heat Exchanger developed using Creo Parametric. The assembly includes the shell, tube bundle, tube sheets, supports, and connection nozzles. The objective was to create a realistic industrial design while maintaining proper assembly relationships and manufacturable geometry.

  • Complete Shell-and-Tube Heat Exchanger Assembly
  • Detailed Internal Tube Bundle Design
  • Industrial CAD Modeling and Assembly Structure
  • Manufacturing-Oriented Component Design
  • Professional Engineering Documentation Ready
