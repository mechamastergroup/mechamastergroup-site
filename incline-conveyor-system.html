Incline Conveyor System

Material Handling Assembly Design

Project Overview

This project showcases the engineering design and development of an Incline Conveyor System, modeled using SolidWorks 2025. The assembly was developed for material handling applications and includes the conveyor structure, cleated belt configuration, drive arrangement, and supporting frame.

The project demonstrates mechanical design capabilities including weldment modeling, assembly creation, motion-oriented design, and engineering visualization. It reflects MechaMaster Group's expertise in industrial equipment design and CAD development.
